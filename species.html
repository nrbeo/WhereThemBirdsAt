Species Information

European Robin

Wingspan: 20–22 cm

Weight: 14–21 g

Lifespan: 8 years

The European Robin is a small insectivorous bird, known for its orange-red breast.

Western Marsh Harrier

Wingspan: 115–130 cm

Weight: 400–800 g

Lifespan: 6 years

A large bird of prey known for its flight and habitat in wetlands.
